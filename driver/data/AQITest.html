Air Quality Index

The U.S. Air Quality Index (AQI) is a color-coded scale from 0 to 500 used to communicate air quality. More about the AQI.

"NowCast" is an estimate of the AQI to show the current air quality. Check your current air quality to see if now is a good time for outdoor activities. More about the NowCast.
